About Me

My name is Kevin Robinson, and I live in the Sunshine State, Florida. I like to create websites and bring them to life through coding and design programs. I've been creating sites since 2004 and started on Proboards. I also know how to create graphics and edit videos from my Multimedia Technology (A.S.) degree.


When I'm not creating magic through web design and development, I like to read, keep up to date with current affairs, play video games, and knit. In my spare time, I volunteer as a league manager for the Nonprofit Organization, CMSA eSports, that has been internationally recognized and twice-nominated for Best LGBTQ+ Contribution to Esports at the Gayming Awards.
